Nutri Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

Nutri is designed to keep meal planning private. Dish search, meal-plan generation, and grocery-list generation run on your device using Apple Foundation Models or a local Gemma model.

Information Nutri handles

Nutri stores the dishes and ingredients you add, meal plans, preferences, grocery lists, assistant choice, and app settings. This content is stored locally. If you enable iCloud, Apple may sync it between your devices or share the lightweight records you explicitly share with another iCloud user. Nutri does not operate a separate meal-data server.

Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat. RevenueCat may receive a pseudonymous app user identifier, product and entitlement status, storefront, app version, and purchase-related diagnostics needed to provide and restore access. Nutri does not receive your full payment-card details.

Diagnostics and third parties

Apple may process device, iCloud, StoreKit, and crash information under your Apple settings and Apple’s policies. RevenueCat processes purchase information as described above. Nutri does not sell personal information and does not use third-party advertising trackers.

Sharing and deletion

Nothing is shared with another person unless you use an iCloud sharing control. You can delete dishes, plans, and lists in the app. Removing the app deletes local data; iCloud data can also be managed through your Apple Account’s iCloud settings.

Children, security, and changes

Nutri is not directed to children under 13. We use platform security controls and minimize transmitted data, but no system can guarantee absolute security. Material policy changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
